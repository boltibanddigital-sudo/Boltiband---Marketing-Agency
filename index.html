FAQs

Common Meta Ads questions.

How much budget do I need to start Meta Ads? +
We recommend a minimum of ₹15,000–₹25,000/month in ad spend to get enough data for meaningful optimisation. Management fees are separate.
Do you handle creatives (videos, images)? +
We write detailed creative briefs your team or a freelancer can execute. Our focus is strategy, targeting, and performance — not video production.
What is CAPI and why does it matter? +
CAPI (Conversions API) is server-side tracking that sends conversion data directly from your server to Meta — bypassing iOS14 browser restrictions. Without it, you lose 30–60% of your conversion signals.
How long before I see results? +
Most campaigns exit the learning phase in 7–14 days. Meaningful CPL improvement typically happens within 3–5 weeks. Unlike SEO, Meta can generate leads within 48 hours of going live.
Do you manage both Facebook and Instagram? +
Yes — both run through Meta Ads Manager. We allocate budget across placements based on where your audience converts most efficiently. Placement decisions are data-driven.

FAQs

Common Google Ads questions.

What budget do I need for Google Ads? +
We recommend a minimum of ₹20,000–₹30,000/month in ad spend. Management fees are separate from ad spend.
Can you take over my existing Google Ads account? +
Yes — we do this often. We start with a full account audit to understand what's working, what's wasting spend, and what's missing.
How is Performance Max different from Search? +
Search targets specific keywords. Performance Max uses AI to serve ads across all Google surfaces. PMax works best with strong creative assets and clear conversion data. We recommend running both.
How quickly can campaigns go live? +
Typically 14–21 days from onboarding to launch — including audit, keyword research, ad copy, tracking setup, and campaign build.
Do you guarantee a specific CPL or ROAS? +
We never guarantee specific numbers — any agency that does is misleading you. We commit to full transparency, continuous optimisation, and honest reporting with complete account access.
